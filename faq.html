FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does it work?
We come to the location and bring the balls, blow them up and then we will come back at the end to take them away, we do not normally stay during the event and then we leave you to play a game of soccer.
Can we have more than x amount of people?
Yes you can have as many people as you like but we do suggest games of 4 on 4 or 5 on 5 and rotating people through because it can get quite tiring.
How much does it cost?
It costs 20 dollars per hour, per ball.
Does the pricing chart include tax?
No the chart does not include tax.
Do you have any suggestions on where we can play?
Yes we can help you with suggestions and suggest places people have played before.
Do you find the venue for us?
Normally that is up to you, as long as there are power outlets then we are good to go but we can help you with suggestions based on places we have been before.
Do you travel to x?
Yes we can travel to most places, however outside of London, standard government mileage rates apply for delivery.
Do you do this for young kids?
Unfortunately we do not have kid size bubbles.
Are there any requirements for the bubbles?
Yes there is a height requirement of 4'11 and an age requirement of 13/14.
What size group do you recommend?
If it is a big enough space, group size does not really matter as everyone can rotate through the balls.
We have been asked by an indoor venue for proof of insurance and TSSA approval permits, can you provide us with those?
We have 2million insurance and after speaking with TSSA our balls are not deemed inflatables and do not need the TSSA permits so we have been told.
How do you usually blow them up if people are renting them for outdoor use?
We need there to be power outlets available, if you live nearby a park we can always come to your house and blow them up there and then you can walk them over.
